INDICATOR
Indirect or ambiguous signals — language that suggests emotional distress or risk but does not explicitly express suicidal or self-harm intent (e.g., expressions of hopelessness, algospeak euphemisms used casually).
IDEATION
Explicit thoughts or wishes about death, suicide, or self-harm, without describing current or past behavior (e.g., "I keep thinking about ending it," "I wish I could just disappear").
BEHAVIOR
Ongoing or recent self-harm behavior described by the author (e.g., cutting, restricting food, substance use as self-harm), without necessarily indicating a suicide attempt.
ATTEMPT
Describes a past or current suicide attempt, or immediate intent to attempt (e.g., "I tried last night," "I have a plan and I'm going to do it tonight").
Not sure / Uncertain
Use when the post is too ambiguous, too short, or otherwise impossible to categorize confidently — even after careful reading.
